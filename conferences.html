Silicon
Valley
Career
Development
Conference

The Silicon Valley Career Development Conference gives DECA members their first taste of a DECA conference. Lynbrook DECA members compete in this conference with all other DECA chapters in the Silicon Valley Area. This competition is just a test for the State-Level competition, so the result here will not affect your participation in States.

California
Career
Development
Conference

The California Career Development Conference is the qualifying competition to see if members are eligible to compete at the International Career Development Conference. This conference is almost the same as the Silicon Valley CDC in all organizational aspects, except members will compete with all other DECA chapters in their state.

International
Career
Development
Conference

This is the largest and most grand conference that DECA has to offer! With over 17,000 people attending each year, the International Career Development Conference is almost a whole week of fun, while meeting people from other countries, competing for the victory, and visiting world-renowned places with friends!
