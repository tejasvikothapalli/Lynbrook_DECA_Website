What is DECA?

DECA is an internationally recognized competitive business club that aims to create opportunities that enhance business knowledge and foster the entrepreneurial spirit amongst high school students. Competitive events span across four career clusters: business management, marketing, finance, and hospitality/tourism. DECA members get the chance to compete in events in these clusters at the regional, state, and international level. All these experiences provide them with an opportunity to network, connect their classroom skills with the real world, and have lots of fun!

Conference

Every school year, Lynbrook DECA offers members the opportunity to attend four distinct conferences: LACE, SVCDC, SCDC, and ICDC. While most conferences are competitive, some are purely learning experiences for DECA members to learn about the various aspects of business through organized events, such as workshops and guest speaker. Regardless of the type of conference, each one provides members with the opportunity to mingle with friends, make new ones, and learn at the same time!

Events

DECA’s competitive events are the highlight of the member experience. These events allow members to apply critical thinking and public speaking skills to their work in a way that isn’t possible in the classroom. DECA’s competitive events span across four career clusters: business management, marketing, finance, and hospitality and tourism. Some events within these clusters require preparation in advance, like the creation of an 11-page business plan, while other events require quick decision making and eloquence under pressure. Regardless, the diversity of events in DECA, from financial analysis to fashion promotion plans, ensures that there is an event for everyone.

Culture

Past all of its educational and competitive aspects, Lynbrook DECA has a rich culture filled with laughs, sarcasm, chapter-wide inside jokes, intense games of Mafia, and friendships spanning across every grade level. Members connect with each other at enrichment opportunities and various social events and gatherings. To enrich business knowledge, Lynbrook DECA organizes speaker events and company tours for a first-hand view into the corporate world. Past speakers include start-up executives and political leaders, and past company tours include Box and LinkedIn. To allow members to also bond in a non-academic setting, Lynbrook DECA hosts socials, like pizza parties at conferences, and an end-of-the-year banquet.
