About Us

Who We Are

Lynbrook DECA is a student-run business organization that offers high school students an opportunity to delve into the areas of marketing, finance, entrepreneurship, etc. DECA teaches members core concepts and prepares them to compete in various events against over 200,000 other DECA members from around the world. Not only do we focus on competing at conferences, but we also provide members with the opportunity to discover business and leadership through community service events, socials, company tours, and speaker events.
