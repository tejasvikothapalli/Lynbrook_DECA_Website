Welcome to Lynbrook DECA!

What is DECA?

Lynbrook DECA is a student-run business organization that offers high school students an opportunity to delve into the areas of marketing, finance, entrepreneurship, etc. DECA teaches members core concepts and prepares them to compete in various events against over 200,000 other DECA members from around the world. Not only do we focus on competing at conferences, but we also provide members with the opportunity to discover/experience business and leadership through community service events, socials, company tours, and speaker events.

Conferences

Every school year, Lynbrook DECA offers members the opportunity to attend four different conferences: LACE, SVCDC, SCDC, and ICDC. While most conferences are competitive, some are purely learning experiences for DECA members to learn through workshops and speaker events. Regardless of the type of conference, each one provides members with the opportunity to mingle with friends, make new ones, and learn while having fun.
