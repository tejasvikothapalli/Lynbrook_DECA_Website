Sign Up for LACE

October 9, 2016

The first conference of the year, LACE, is finally here! It takes place during November 11-13, and is a purely NON-COMPETITIVE CONFERENCE. This means that the conference is for DECA members to learn about the club, attend valuable workshops, listen to world-renowned guest speakers, network with other DECA members, and get valuable tips about their own competitive events for future conferences. If you are interested in attending, please sign up on this form. The cost of the conference is $180 (make check payable to lynbrook ASB), and the deadline to sign up and turn in all forms is October 13th. If you have any questions, feel free to contact vpoperations@lynbrookdeca.org.

Demo Day

September 12, 2016

Make sure to come out to our DEMO DAY, this Wednesday (9/14) from 3-5:30pm in the cafeteria! We will be explaining the different types of competitive events that are available to our members at conferences. This is a great opportunity to get a feel for the competitive aspect of our club as well as meet our officer and cluster captain team!
This event is STRONGLY recommended for our new members, but will be beneficial to returning members as well in refreshing your memory and helping you choose your events for this upcoming year. We hope to see you all there!

Stock Market Game

September 9, 2016

Ever wanted to experience real-time trading of the stock market? This year's DECA Stock Market Game gives that exact opportunity; along with the chance to qualify to ICDC 2017 in Anaheim! Because this event does not count as your written nor your roleplay, you can still compete in two events at both SVCDC and States.
This simulation finishes on December 16th, 2016, and the top 25 competitors of each of the four regions-Central, Southern, Western (Our division), and North-Atlantic-will be able to compete at ICDC in this event. Be sure to send an e-mail to onlinecc@lynbrookdeca.org if you're interested!
