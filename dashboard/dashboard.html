Idea Challenge

November 15, 2015

A premier event of Global Entrepreneurship Week (GEW), the DECA Idea Challenge 2015 is a fast-paced, hands-on learning experience that dares students around the globe to generate an innovative new use for a commonplace item in just eight days. Students must organize into teams of 3-5 people, access the challenge item online, collaborate as a team to produce an original and sustainable use for the item and pitch their invention in an engaging YouTube video presentation.

The mystery item is cardboard boxes. Sign up here.

Virtual Business Challenge

October 17, 2015

The DECA Virtual Business Challenge is an official DECA competitive event where participants use highly visual simulation software to manage a business. The DECA Virtual Business Challenge (VBC) encourages DECA members to test their skills at managing a business individually or as a team. The VBC culminates in live competitions at the ICDC Nationals. This year's VBC will have the six tracks, VBC Accounting, VBC Restaurant, VBC Retailing, VBC Sports, VBC Personal Finance and the DECA Hotel Challenge.

Click HERE to access the Virtual Business Challenge webpage where you will be able to sign up on your own. Once you are on there website click on "Register for Competitions" and follow their steps to sign up. Then allow our advisor to approve your account. If you have any questions please email directortesting@lynbrookdeca.org.
