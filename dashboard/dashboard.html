Virtual Business Challenge

October 17, 2015

The DECA Virtual Business Challenge is an official DECA competitive event where participants use highly visual simulation software to manage a business. The DECA Virtual Business Challenge (VBC) encourages DECA members to test their skills at managing a business individually or as a team. The VBC culminates in live competitions at the ICDC Nationals. This year's VBC will have the six tracks, VBC Accounting, VBC Restaurant, VBC Retailing, VBC Sports, VBC Personal Finance and the DECA Hotel Challenge.

Click HERE to access the Virtual Business Challenge webpage where you will be able to sign up on your own. Once you are on there website click on "Register for Competitions" and follow their steps to sign up. Then allow our advisor to approve your account. If you have any questions please email directortesting@lynbrookdeca.org

Sign Up for LACE

October 1, 2015

The first conference of the year, LACE, is finally here! It takes place during November 13-15, and is a purely NON-COMPETITIVE CONFERENCE. This means that the conference is for DECA members to learn about the club, attend valuable workshops, listen to world-renowned guest speakers, network with other DECA members, and get valuable tips about their own competitive events for future conferences. If you are interested in attending, please sign up on this form. The cost of the conference is $220, and the deadline to sign up and turn in all forms is October 16th. If you have any questions, feel free to contact vpoperations@lynbrookdeca.org.

Stock Market Game

September 1, 2015

Hi everybody! Just like last year, there will be a DECA Stock Market Game beginning September 8th. You’ll be tasked with managing a virtual investment portfolio of $100,000 in groups of 1-3 for three months, and the top 25 finalists in the western United States region will have the opportunity to go and compete at ICDC! We encourage all members to participate, because there is nothing to lose by taking part. Shoot an email ASAP to directortesting@lynbrookdeca.org if you’re interested in participating!
