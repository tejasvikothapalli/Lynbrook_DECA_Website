Sign Up for LACE

October 1, 2015

The first conference of the year, LACE, is finally here! It is from November 13-15, and is a NON-COMPETITIVE CONFERENCE. This conference is for DECA members to learn about the club, as well as attend valuable workshops, listen to world-renowned guest speakers, network with other DECA members, and gain valuable tips about their own competitive events. If you are interested, please sign up on this form. The cost of the conference is $220, and the the deadline to sign up is October 16th. If you have any questions, feel free to contact vpoperations@lynbrookdeca.org


New Member Workshop

September 19, 2015

Lynbrook DECA is excited to host our Intro to DECA Workshop on September 25th. The event will take place from 3:00 p.m. to 5:30 p.m. in the Lynbrook Cafeteria. New members are encouraged to attend to learn more about DECA and its competitive events as well as to get to know the officer and cluster captain teams. We will also be playing games and food will be provided. Hope to see you all there!


Friday Meetings

September 13, 2015

Thank you to all those who came out to our first meeting in the auditorium last Tuesday. We hope you're all excited for a great year in Lynbrook DECA! From here on out, we will be having our regular meetings on FRIDAYS AT LUNCH IN ROOM 608. Our second meeting will be this Friday (9/18), and we recommend that everyone comes to these meetings because we will be covering important information relating to logistics, events, deadlines, and much more. However, we understand that some of you can't make it due to other clubs or commitments. In that case, make sure to read the emails that we send out and contact an officer if you have any further questions. If you have not recieved any Lynbrook DECA emails so far, please email presidents@lynbrookdeca.org and let us know to add you onto our mailing list.


Stock Market Game

September 1, 2015

Hi everybody! Just like last year, there will be a DECA Stock Market Game beginning September 8th. You’ll be tasked with managing a virtual investment portfolio of $100,000 in groups of 1-3 for three months, and the top 25 finalists in the western United States region will have the opportunity to go and compete at ICDC! We encourage all members to participate, because there is nothing to lose by taking part. Shoot an email ASAP to directortesting@lynbrookdeca.org if you’re interested in participating!


Flash Mob!

August 31, 2015

Hey everyone!! For club promotion this year, Lynbrook DECA is planning on doing a flashmob in the middle of the quad on September 11th! We will be dancing to the song "Watch Me" by Silento. We already have approximately 30 people signed up, so if you would like to join in on the fun, sign up here. More information will be sent to those who sign up on the spreadsheet!
