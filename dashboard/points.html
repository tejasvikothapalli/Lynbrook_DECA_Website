DECA Diamonds

In order to keep yourself accountable during competitive preparation season, you will receive DECA Diamonds if you complete certain goals. This will allow you to track your progress. DECA Diamonds can be earned by completing practice role-plays, meeting deadlines, or creating study guides. At the end of the year, you will only be eligible to apply for an officer position if you have met ______ points.

Cord Points

In order to receive a graduation cord in senior year from DECA, you must meet a point requirement to illustrate your active membership. These points can be earned by attending meetings, study sessions, conferences, socials, and other enrichment activities.
