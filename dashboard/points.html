DECA Diamonds

In order to keep yourself accountable during competitive preparation season, you will receive "DECA Diamonds" upon completing certain tasks. This spreadsheet will allow you to track your progress. DECA Diamonds can be earned by completing practice role-plays, meeting competitive deadlines, attending various club events, etc. Every meeting or study session, the officers will announce if there is a new task that can be completed for DECA Diamonds. At the end of the year, you will only be eligible to apply for an officer position if you possess a minimum of 125 DECA Diamonds.
Click here for more information about DECA Diamond distribution per activity.

Cord Points

In order to receive a graduation cord from Lynbrook DECA during your senior year graduation, you must meet a certain cord point requirement to illustrate your active membership within the club. These cord points can be earned by attending meetings, study sessions, conferences, socials, and all the other enrichment activities that Lynbrook DECA offers.
